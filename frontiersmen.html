Frontiersmen

aenygma

The best poetry (art?) (unfolds itself in fighting) is made when you can’t find (locate) in the world what you so desperately need.

In writing the loss and in (voicing, wailing?) speaking the eulogy, and deeply mourning, we bring about what we needed.

(Joys?)

And most importantly, in that final turn, realize (realizing?) (a pain fully felt is never yours) the pain was never yours.

(It is fleeting)

Just a way for you to create; terraform and leave behind the breadcrumbs (compost, tree motif) for what’s (whatever’s) to come.
